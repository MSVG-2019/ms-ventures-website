The group

A private investment group, built to hold for generations

M&S Ventures Group is a privately owned investment and holding group, established in 2019 and based in Montreux. It is wholly owned by its founder, Milind Madan, and invests only its own capital. It retains full, independent control of its investment decisions.

The group holds three kinds of assets: Swiss real estate, controlling stakes in Swiss companies, and investments in public securities worldwide. Its manner across all three is the same. It is a patient, principal owner — it carefully commits capital, then steps back, leaving each building, business and holding to the leaders who know it best.

Swiss Real Estate

A long-held portfolio of residential and conversion property on the Lake Geneva Riviera — owned for the long term and cared for, on the group's behalf, by established Swiss partners.

Stakes in Swiss Companies

Controlling and strategic shareholdings in Swiss businesses, held as a patient owner. Each company is run by its own management; the group provides capital, permanence and discretion.

Global Securities

Long-term positions in listed companies and securities around the world — a quiet, diversified allocation, held through cycles and measured in years.
